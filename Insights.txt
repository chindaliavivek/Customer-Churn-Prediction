Customer Churn Prediction – Key Insights

1. Contract Type
   - Month-to-month customers show the highest churn.
   - One-year and two-year contracts retain customers far better.

2. Tenure
   - Tenure is the strongest predictor.
   - Customers who stayed >20 months rarely churn.

3. Internet Service
   - Fiber Optic users churn more than DSL users.
   - Indicates possible pricing or performance dissatisfaction.

4. Payment Method
   - Electronic Check customers have extremely high churn.
   - Automatic payments reduce churn.

5. Monthly Charges
   - Higher monthly charges → higher churn probability.
   - Indicates price-sensitive customers.

6. Senior Citizens
   - Slightly higher churn likelihood.

7. Paperless Billing
   - Shows a moderate association with churn.

Business Recommendations:
- Offer discounts or loyalty perks for Month-to-Month customers.
- Target high charge customers with retention offers.
- Improve Fiber Optic customer experience.
- Incentivize Auto-Pay systems to reduce churn.
